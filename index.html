About the Program

The Saudi Sports for All Federation (SFA) organizes the Neighborhood Clubs Program with direct support from the Ministry of Sport, Ministry of Education, and Quality of Life Program to fulfill Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 goals. The Neighborhood Clubs offer sport, educational, and recreational activities and programs for people of all ages and abilities. The program strives to encourage students and their families to adopt a healthy to boost physical and mental wellbeing through physical activity.

The program also seeks to uplift local communities by offering volunteering and training opportunities in the Neighborhood facilities. Registration will be available through the neighborhood club’s website or by visiting the nearest neighborhood club. After that, we invite members to take an introductory tour inside the club’s facilities and pay the registration fees at the facility.

The membership categories include:

  • 10 SAR for daily entry tickets for all ages
  • 40 SAR per month for children aged 6 -15 years old
  • 60 SAR per month for people over 15 years old
  • 120 SAR per month for small families (a parent and two kids or less)
  • 200 SAR per month for families of a parent and more than two kids
